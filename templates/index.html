SELECTING RESEARCH TOPICS EFFECTIVELY

Problem statement

This project is geared towards young researchers such as undergraduates motivated to pursue graduate school. Selecting a research topic which might lead to a thesis can be a daunting challenge. There are many factors to be considered. While personal interests and experience matter, a balance must be struck with the currently available opportunities and future promise in any particular field. Within a field, being aware of rising trends, for e.g. 'Machine learning' in Physics, is also extremely important.

Solution

Using the arxiv database for physics, I have compared the buzz words in all of physics between two different years. We can see some emerging trends such as 'neural networks' and 'machine learning' as well as some topics that have stayed both stable and prolific over the years such as 'optics'.

A bar chart provided below compares the most active fields within physics between two different years. Comparing the 2015 and 2020 research trends, we see that topics such as Neural Networks, Machine Learning, and COVID(!?) are currently trending, and seem promising for the near future.
